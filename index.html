About

Gabby Brown is a bilingual lifestyle and fashion creator known for her romantic, hyper-feminine aesthetic and love of all things pink, whimsical, and vintage-inspired. Through dress hauls, thrifted treasures, styling diaries, and everyday lifestyle moments, she invites her audience into a world of coquette fashion, fairytale charm, and modern femininity.

With a warm and engaging voice, Gabby creates content that feels both aspirational and approachable—blending dreamy visuals, storytelling, and personal style into a digital diary that resonates with women who love beauty, fashion, and a little everyday magic.
